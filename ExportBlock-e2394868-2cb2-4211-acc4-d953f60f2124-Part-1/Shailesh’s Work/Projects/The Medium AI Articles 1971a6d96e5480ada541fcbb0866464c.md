# The Medium AI Articles

Text: Series of Medium Posts to explain how current AI works in layman terms.

## Context

I started writing these because:

1. I love teaching 
2. writing these posts would force me to understand this stuff deeply
3. I wanted to create a body of work which proved to people that I knew about AI.

The posts have stopped since I while because I was prepping for my wedding in the second half of the last year and since then I have been doing more hands-on AI stuff, than theoretical AI stuff. Still, this body of work can be incredibly valuable to people who want a top-level understanding of LLMs.

## Posts

1. https://medium.com/@shaileshrana1995/ai-simplified-machine-learning-basics-f579a90ddd0e

This is my favourite post because it simplifies neural networks to very, very fundamental principals. This is probably also the best written post since I had years to understand and simplify my understanding of Neural Networks, as opposed to the Transformer Architecture which I only understood about a year ago.

Highly recommended for anyone getting into AI.

1. https://medium.com/@shaileshrana1995/ai-explained-how-to-teach-an-old-computer-new-words-61d65a41e1b5

This is an extremely simple and intuitive explanation of how Transformers (the T in ChatGPT) work. It is incredibly intuitive as I take examples from the english language and distill the main concept of attention and relate it to simple grammatical rules.

In fairness, this is highly inspired by this video by 3Blue1Brown, but my explanation covers more breadth and is simpler.

[https://youtu.be/wjZofJX0v4M?si=0LyS4QWI6u_rZ8NB](https://youtu.be/wjZofJX0v4M?si=0LyS4QWI6u_rZ8NB)

1. https://medium.com/@shaileshrana1995/ai-explained-objects-as-vectors-83c6c40c843f

I regard this post as chump change and as sort of a filler since this is probably the shorted post and was the quickest to write. However, ironically, this is the most useful post for anyone doing knowledge work since this applies to any field, including life itself.

This is essentially a framework for thinking and very valuable to adopt in real life.

1. https://medium.com/@shaileshrana1995/the-endgame-of-llms-virtual-employees-617071df4403

This is the plan to create a true AI Agent, even before Agents were all the hype (this was written in August 2024).

The execution plan and the techniques are completely obsolete. In fact, since the field of AI moves so quickly, the techniques became obsolete like 15 days after publishing.

But the vision is grand and true and remains unconquered. I love that the vision was so big.

I tried and failed to implement this, but that’s okay.
